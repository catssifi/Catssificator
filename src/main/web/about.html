Missionary
Think about it would be nice to have an automatic intelligent system that can classify all your search input into different categories without having a nightmare to develop one on your own (risks/resources/budgets..etc ?). Your first thought of solution might be Google - well, however, this is not a nice free lunch. Once you send them lot of 'robot' alike queries, they are just smart enough to figure out you are not a normal user and will block your IP in a second. Therefore, it is always our mission to provide an free open-source A.I. system that can do this job right for you.
